THIRTY SECOND STORIES

Thirty Second Stories is a collaborative project. User-submitted stories are combined with audio and visuals and then collected into ten-story episodes. The playback of the stories is randomized. If you would like to submit a story for the next episode, please drop me a quick line at ZachRScott@gmail.com and I'll reply with details.

Concept, visuals, sounds: Zach Scott
Code: Kyle Demeule (link?)
Idea-bouncing: Chris Roeleveld

This -youtube playlist- contains storyteller credits as well as higher resolution versions of each video which can be easily shared.

Follow me on Twitter to be notified when new episodes are added. If you like Thirty Second Stories, you might also enjoy my earlier projects ConferenceCall.biz and Introspections.me.
